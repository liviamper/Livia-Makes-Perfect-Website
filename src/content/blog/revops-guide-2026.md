Many businesses treat Sales, Marketing, and Customer Success as separate islands. In 2026, those silos are where revenue goes to die. 

**Revenue Operations (RevOps)** is the bridge. It’s the strategy of aligning your data, your tools (like HubSpot and Salesforce), and your people to ensure your customer journey is frictionless.

### The Three Pillars of RevOps at Livia:
1. **Data Integrity:** Ensuring your CRM isn't a mess of duplicates and outdated leads.
2. **Process Automation:** Letting software handle the boring follow-ups so your humans can focus on closing deals.
3. **Transparent Reporting:** Knowing exactly which dollar spent on Meta Ads resulted in a dollar of profit.

At **Livia Makes Perfect**, we specialize in auditing your current mess and turning it into a streamlined revenue machine.

> "If you can't measure it, you can't manage it." — The RevOps Mantra.
